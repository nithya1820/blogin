About Us

We started our website Blogin to make a one-stop page to read and post blogs for people interested in food, travel, and education. The main features of the website are that the user can blog content, read blogs, and the readers can comment and react to blogs made, this makes the whole experience more interactive for the reader and the blogger bringing users from different diversities to share their ideas on topics related to the topics mentioned previously. Blogin brings people from different places and creates a platform to share their experience with food related to their culture and country, and travel enthusiasts might have a chance to connect with each other via the comment section, and as far as education is concerned users could post interesting facts and theories that might interest young minds.
